# Data Analytics Job Market Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes real-world job listings to understand trends in the data analytics and data science job market.
The goal is to extract meaningful insights related to skills, salary, experience, location, and company hiring patterns.

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Scikit-learn  

---

## Dataset

- 7,500+ job listings  
- Features include:
  - Job Title  
  - Company Name  
  - Location  
  - Experience  
  - Salary  
  - Skills  

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Skills Analysis
- Identified most in-demand skills
- Machine Learning, Python, SQL are dominant

---

### 2. Experience vs Salary
- Salary increases significantly with experience
- Growth is non-linear

---

### 3. Location-Based Demand
- Bengaluru is the top hiring hub
- Followed by Hyderabad, Chennai, and Gurugram
- Remote jobs are also significant

---

### 4. Company Analysis
- High hiring companies: Accenture, Turing  
- High paying companies: Goldman Sachs, Infineon  

---

## Final Insights

- Advanced skills like Machine Learning increase salary potential  
- Experience plays a major role in salary growth  
- Job opportunities are concentrated in major tech hubs  
- There is a clear difference between high hiring and high paying companies  
- Global opportunities indicate strong demand for data professionals  

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can be used to understand real-world job market trends and support informed career decisions.
